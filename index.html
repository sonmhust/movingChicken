📖 Cambridge Maths Glossary
📐Rotation: turning a shape about a fixed point by a given angle.
↩️Clockwise: direction of rotation same as clock hands (right → down → left → up).
↪️Anticlockwise: opposite direction (right → up → left → down).
📏Right angle: an angle of exactly 90°, forming a quarter turn.
🧭Direction / Bearing: the orientation in which the chicken is facing.
📦Grid / Array: a rectangular arrangement of rows and columns.
📍Coordinates (column, row): the position of a cell in the grid.
